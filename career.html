Careers

In Civie, we value integrity and trust in our relationships. Part of achieving exceptional operational service for our customers is the training and guidance of our employees, which is the forefront of Civie's foundation.

We share our vision with our employees clearly because our business can only thrive when our people do. We are always looking for new talented and diligent individuals - who has what it takes to take on new challenges in the industry, or benefit from a strong mentorship and build new skills from our line of work.

Together, we can strive to offer value to Cebu's construction services and technical expertise.

The following positions are available in the Operations Department:

-Truck Drivers
-Backhoe Operators
-Crane Operators
-Rigger
-Wheel Loader Operators

The following positions are available in the Repair and Maintenance Department:

-Welders
-Mechanics
-Assistance Mechanics
-Carpenters

The following positions are available in the Administration Department:

-Accountants
-Operation Officer
-Office Assistant
-Messenger


For all inquires or interest about our job openings, please call us or come by to our office with your employee documentation and fill out an Application Form, we can also be reached out through our email at: sales@civieengineering.com.
